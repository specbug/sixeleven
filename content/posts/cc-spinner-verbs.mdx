Claude Code lets you replace the default loading messages with custom ones. It's called [`spinnerVerbs`](https://www.threads.com/@boris_cherny/post/DUB4mtmEskQ), purely cosmetic, zero utility, I love it.

You're heads-down debugging something, grinding through it, and then "Quirrelling" flashes by. Or "Simply walking in". Or "Just as planned" appears right before Claude suggests exactly what you were hoping for. It snaps you out of the trance for a second, brings a smile, and you carry on.
